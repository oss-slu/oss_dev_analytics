# Client Onboarding Questions

## Overview
These questions are meant to help us understand what a team actually needs from the OSS Dev Analytics dashboard. The goal is to make sure the dashboard is useful and shows insights that actually matter to them.

## Project Goals
- What are your main goals for using this dashboard?
- What does success look like for your team?
- Are there any specific problems you’re dealing with right now?

## Metrics and Insights
- Which metrics matter most to you? (like velocity, blocked work, team satisfaction)
- Are there any metrics you already track that you’d want included?
- Do you care more about trends over time or just where things are at right now?

## Team Workflow
- How is your team set up?
- How do you currently track work? (GitHub, Trello, Notion, etc.)
- How often does your team meet or check in?
- How do you usually deal with blockers?

## Actionable Insights
- When a metric is low, what do you usually do?
- What kind of suggestions would actually be helpful to you?
- Are there any resources (docs, links, tools) you’d want quick access to?

## Tools and Integration
- What tools does your team use? (GitHub, Slack, Discord, etc.)
- Would you want the dashboard to connect with any of these?
- Do you already have documentation or workflows we should know about?

## Customization and Preferences
- Do you want to choose which metrics are shown?
- How often should the data update?
- Is there anything specific you’d want the dashboard to highlight?

## Data and Access
- Which repositories or organizations do you want included?
- Do you already have access set up for this data?
- Are there any privacy or data concerns we should know about?

## Alerts and Notifications
- Would you want to be notified if certain metrics drop or change?
- If yes, how would you want to be notified? (Slack, email, etc.)

## Stakeholder Perspective
- Who is the main stakeholder for this project?
- What are their main goals or expectations?
- What kind of updates or reports do they usually expect?
- How often do they check in on progress?
- What metrics or outcomes matter most to them?
- Are there any specific concerns or priorities they have?

## Additional Feedback
- Is there anything else you’d want from a dashboard like this?
- Have you seen any features elsewhere that you really liked?
